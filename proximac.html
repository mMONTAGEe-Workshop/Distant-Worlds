Description:
The abundant metal-rich rings encircling Proxima Centauri C have proven instrumental in the developmental strides of the Novus Genesis colony. At the forefront of this resource utilization is an orbital refinery starport strategically positioned in close proximity to the primary ring, serving as the central hub for the refinement of exotic metals extracted from the mining operations.

Orbiting at a much greater distance is an orbital assembler dedicated to the manufacturing of ULT Type-8 Asteroid Miners and cargo haulers. This assembly facility, stationed in the outer reaches of the system, ensures the efficient production of the advanced machinery essential for the ongoing mining endeavors and the sustained growth of Novus Genesis. The orchestrated synergy between these orbital installations underscores the colony's commitment to maximizing the potential of its celestial surroundings for the benefit of its thriving community.
