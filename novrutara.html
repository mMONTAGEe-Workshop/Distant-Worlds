Description:
Novrutara, a temperate marine terra, serves as the headquarters for the Settled Exoplanets Defense Treaty (SEDF). The thriving Novrut city, the largest and most settled on the planet, emerged as the chosen settlement after Revolutionary Rebels hijacking several Kepler Class Interstellar Cruiser Model 2 from Lunar Docks with defectors joining from other colonies. The rebels strategically opted for Novrutara due to its farthest distance that Model 2 can travel. Chandra, an asteroid moon orbiting Sinara, functions primarily as an automated military complex, housing subterranean military factories and complexes within Sinara moon itself.

The enriched soil of Novrutara has facilitated the establishment of expansive farms, propelling the growth of the agricultural economy across settled exoplanets. The Sinara SO2 seas and lakes, with their antimicrobial properties, Sulphur Dioxide is extracted and serve as preservatives, preventing spoilage caused by microorganisms. Additionally, their antioxidant properties inhibit chemical and enzymatic oxidation, preserving the freshness and appearance of food and averting undesirable darkening.
